About the Conference

A One-Day Journey Into the Power of Language

The Language Advantage Conference (TLAC) is a dynamic one-day experience in Lagos, Nigeria, designed to show how multilingualism drives education, careers, and cultural innovation, locally and globally.

1

Discover

Explore how languages, foreign, African, and heritage, create access to education, global careers, and cross-border opportunities.

2

Learn

Engage in thought-provoking discussions and hands-on workshops on teaching, translation, global business, AI, EdTech, and modern language learning.

3

Connect

Network with educators, learners, innovators, and organizations shaping the future of language, culture, and global communication.

4

Transform

Leave TLAC with clarity, tools, and connections to turn your language skills into real-world opportunities and lasting impact.

Meet the Founder

The Vision Behind TLAC

Favour Fashina

Visionary Founder, SprachVille International

Favour Fashina is the driving force behind The Language Advantage Conference (TLAC) and the founder of SprachVille International, a language institute based in Lagos, Nigeria.

With a deep passion for unlocking global opportunities through language, Favour has dedicated her career to empowering individuals and organizations to communicate effectively across cultures.

As the visionary behind TLAC, she imagined a platform where Africa leads the global conversation on language, culture, and opportunity, inspiring learners, educators, and innovators to see multilingualism as a strategic advantage.

“Language is more than words, it’s a bridge to opportunity, connection, and global impact. TLAC was born to bring that vision to life.”
